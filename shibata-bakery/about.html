About

Started in Oxford, Ohio, Shibata Bakery (or 柴田のパン屋 'Shibata no Panya' in Japanese) is a small cottage-food bakery run by Kaylynn Borror.

Kaylynn has studied Japanese for more than five years, having lived in Nagoya, Japan for a few months in 2019. Ever since coming back (and missing the different breads and pastries that became a staple of her breakfast diet) she's experimented with making various Japanese baked goods, along with branching out to pastries from other Southeast Asian countries.
